It is the realization of dreams and aspirations of many illustrious educationists and enthusiastic parents for a school, which teaches right living, spiritual advancement, and code of conduct, self-control, patriotism and nonviolence, besides excelling in all educational endeavours.
At Kashmir Valley School, students are encouraged and supported to be creative, reflective, and value based motivated learners. Individual talents of the children are spotted, nourished and encouraged. Along with rigorous academic programme, training in a variety of co-curricular activities helps the students empower themselves with holistic education, preparing them to be successful leaders for better tomorrow.
The campus is being developed in to a the CBSE centre of learning, with state of the art facilities, commodious classrooms, fully equipped science, maths and language labs to prepare the pupils with hands on experiments, a variety of club and co-curricular activities for them to be trained to be better conscious leaders of tomorrow.
Deeply devoted teachers, who dream of a meaningful future for their students, infuse the spirit of scientific enquiry and fashion them into sensitive, sensible and responsible world citizens of the next generation.
